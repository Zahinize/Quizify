BIG BUMPER ROUND

  • For every correct answer, +100 will be rewarded. For every wrong answer, -50 will be deducted from the team's current score.
  • Every selected team must have to play the round. There is no option to pass or skip it. Choose wisely before raising your hand.
  • No answer options available for the round. Correct answer needs to be verbally communicated to the organiser within given time frame.
  • 20 seconds will be given to tell the correct answer.

In case of a tie, a Super Quiz Wizard round will be organised and one chosen member from each team will participate for a best of 5 round to win.
